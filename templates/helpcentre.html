Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI model?
Our hybrid AI predictor achieves an accuracy of approximately 94% on standard MRI datasets. However, it should be used as a supplementary tool and not a definitive diagnosis.
What image formats are supported?
We currently support JPEG, PNG, BMP, and TIFF formats. Images are automatically resized and normalized for the predictive model.
Are my scans secure?
Absolutely. All uploads are processed securely using AES-256 encryption. We comply with GDPR and HIPAA guidelines regarding data privacy and the right to erasure.
