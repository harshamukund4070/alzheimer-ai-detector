Patient Rights & Data Ethics

Right to Privacy

Users have the absolute right to know how their MRI data is stored and who can access it. All scans are encrypted using AES-256 standards.

Informed Consent

No analysis is performed without explicit patient consent. You have the right to withdraw your data at any time from our neural database.

Transparency

Patients are entitled to a full explanation of AI results. Our Grad-CAM heatmaps ensure the AI's diagnostic logic is visible and explainable.

Right to Erasure

Under HIPAA and GDPR guidelines, you can request the permanent deletion of your MRI records and clinical history from this platform.
